How I Work

Product Skills

Product & Delivery

I translate ambiguity into practical next steps: shaping requirements, prioritising backlogs, supporting sprint rituals, running demos, and helping teams understand not just what to build, but why it matters.

Technical Communication

With a full-stack development background, I bridge conversations between users, stakeholders, developers, QA, support, and leadership — keeping enough technical detail without losing the human problem.

UX & Human-Centred Thinking

I focus on clarity, accessibility, emotional context, and reducing friction in moments where users need confidence. Good UX is often the difference between a feature existing and a feature being trusted.

QA, Detail & Systems Thinking

I enjoy spotting patterns, finding edge cases, testing assumptions, and turning scattered feedback into structured improvements. Good product work is part map-making, part detective work.

About Me

A product generalist with technical roots and a human-centred compass.

My background spans product supervision, full-stack web development, technical support, software engineering, teaching, and volunteer translation. That mix means I’m comfortable with both the people side and the technical side of product work.

I’m especially interested in products that help people understand what’s happening, what they can do next, and why it matters. I care about accessibility, emotional clarity, thoughtful systems, and building tools that make complicated situations feel more manageable.
